About Me

Hello, I am Alexandra Dela Cruz, a 20-year-old second-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at the University of Mindanao. I am currently navigating a phase of growth where academics, personal development, and purpose come together, shaping not only who I am as a student but also who I am becoming as an individual. My journey is grounded in curiosity, resilience, and a deep desire to build a future in technology while staying connected to creativity, leadership, and self-expression.

My academic journey was enriched by the support of the SM Foundation, which awarded me a scholarship that allowed me to focus on my studies and explore my passion for technology.

Throughout my college years, I embraced leadership opportunities that shaped my personal and professional development. I became involved in various school organizations, gaining experiences that helped shape my growth and perspective, even though I eventually chose to step back and resign to focus on my personal goals and academic priorities. Additionally, I was honored to participate in the "Futuresmart Leaders: A P.L.U.S. Factor Program," a four-year leadership program designed to empower young leaders like myself.

Beyond my academic and leadership pursuits, I have a deep passion for reading and writing and taking myself on long walks. These hobbies not only provide me with joy and inspiration but also help me articulate my thoughts and share my experiences with others. Photography is another interest of mine, allowing me to capture the beauty of everyday moments and express my creativity through a different medium.

As I continue my journey in the field of technology, and generally, my life (maybe?), I look forward to sharing insights, experiences, and stories through this blog. Join me as I explore the intersections of technology, leadership, and personal growth, and I hope to inspire others along the way.
